Making the Mochi-

Ingredients:
1）Glutinous rice flour- 100g
2）Corn starch – 25g
3）Sugar – 30g
4）Salt – Pinch
5）Milk – 170g
6）Cooking Oil – 15g
7）Nature food color powder 
8）Cooked flour
9）Taro paste – 300g (divide into 10 portions, 30g each)


Making the Taro-

Ingredients：
1）Taro – 300g（after skin is removed)
2）Granulated sugar- 60g
3）All purpose flour – 1/2 tsp
4）Water – 100g
5）Cooking oil – 25g
